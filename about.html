Welcome!

Alissa Moore is the owner and operator of Personal Best Fitness. She has been training in the Louisville/Southern Indiana area since 1999. Her passion of fitness and desire to help others began during her time spent as a high school and collegiate cheerleader and powerlifter.

Alissa holds a Masters degree in Exercise Physiology from the University of Louisville and an undergraduate degree in Sports Medicine from the University of Evansville. She is a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist, certified by the National Strength and Conditioning Association.

Alissa believes that education is what keeps her and her programs growing, enabling her to create diverse workouts that are both challenging and fun. She trains individuals from age 4 to 104, and can help those who need "special" care, from either a recent injury, or from a life-long condition.

Alissa is married to Eric Moore and they have two children, Wyatt and Lila, who can often be seen around the studio. They help create the family atmosphere that Alissa strives to have at Personal Best.

Browse our site to learn about our services and contact us to see how we can help you become your personal best.
